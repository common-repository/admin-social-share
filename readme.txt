== Description ==

This plugin is a simple way for multi-author WordPress blogs to encourage their contributing authors to socially share their content once it has been published to the most popular social networks, including: Twitter, Google+, Facebook, and LinkedIn.  Any time that a post or page who’s status is set to to publish is created or updated, the author will be prompted to share their new article via the social networks.  As social signals are a critical SEO ranking factor and can offer significant increases in referral traffic, this plugin is a must for any WordPress webmaster who has multiple contributors and/or who is interested in efficiency.

This plugin was created by [Avvo](http://www.avvo.com/ "Avvo"), **[a free legal advice website](http://www.avvo.com/free-legal-advice "a free legal advice website")** that provides professional legal advice from more than 120,000 active attorneys in the United States.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `admin-social-share.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. That's it! Any time a post or page is created or updated, back-end users will see social sharing widgets.
